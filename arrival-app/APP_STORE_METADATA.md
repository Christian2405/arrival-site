## Description

Arrival is an AI-powered field assistant built for HVAC technicians, plumbers, electricians, and builders. It combines voice commands, camera analysis, and deep trade knowledge to help you work faster and smarter on every job.

**Talk to it hands-free**
Ask questions while your hands are busy. Arrival understands trade terminology and gives you specific, practical answers — not textbook theory. Wire sizing, refrigerant pressures, error codes, diagnostic steps — all by voice.

**Point your camera at the problem**
In Job Mode, Arrival watches through your camera and proactively alerts you to issues it spots — corrosion, incorrect wiring, code violations, leak indicators. It's like having a second set of experienced eyes on every job.

**Error code lookup**
Browse error codes for every major brand — Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Rinnai, AO Smith, Mitsubishi, and more. Get the code meaning, common causes, and fix steps instantly.

**Upload your manuals**
Upload equipment manuals and Arrival can reference them when answering your questions. Your team's documents become searchable AI knowledge.

**Save answers for later**
Bookmark useful responses so you can pull them up on the next job without asking again.

**Built for the field**
- Works with one hand or no hands (full voice control)
- Dark interface designed for bright outdoor conditions
- Fast responses — no waiting around on a ladder
- Speaks answers back so you can keep working

Arrival is built by tradespeople who understand that you need real answers, not disclaimers. It won't tell you to "consult a professional" — you ARE the professional.

## App Review Notes

Arrival uses the device camera to analyze equipment photos and provide diagnostic assistance to trade professionals. The microphone is used for hands-free voice interaction — users speak questions and receive spoken answers.

**Camera usage**: Users point their phone camera at HVAC equipment, electrical panels, plumbing systems, etc. The app captures frames and uses AI vision to identify potential issues, read model/serial numbers, and provide contextual guidance.

**Microphone usage**: Voice is the primary interaction method since technicians often have both hands occupied. Speech is transcribed and sent to our API for processing.

**AI disclosure**: This app uses AI (Claude by Anthropic) to generate responses. Users are aware they are interacting with an AI assistant.
