What does 3 blinks mean on this Rheem furnace?
That's a pressure switch fault. Check the condensate drain first — blow it out with compressed air. If it's clear, check the inducer motor for bearing noise and inspect the hose for cracks.
What wire size do I need for a 40 amp circuit?
8 AWG copper. If your run is over 50 feet, bump to 6 AWG for voltage drop.
