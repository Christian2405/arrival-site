The Problem

Not enough skilled workers to fill them.

02
The Solution

An AI assistant that answers
any trade question in seconds

  • Ask by voice, text, or camera — answers in ~5 seconds
  • 421 verified error codes across 19 brands
  • Reads equipment labels, model numbers, and blink codes from camera
  • Upload company manuals — AI answers from your docs
What does 3 blinks mean on this Rheem furnace?
Pressure switch fault. Check condensate drain first — blow it out with compressed air.
What wire size for a 40 amp circuit?
8 AWG copper. Over 50 feet, bump to 6 AWG for voltage drop.
03
Flagship Feature

Job Mode

  • Tech activates Job Mode — sets phone down, keeps working
  • Camera stays on. AI watches continuously via LiveKit WebRTC
  • AI speaks up proactively when it spots something wrong
  • Safety hazards, code violations, corrosion, wrong fittings
  • Full-duplex — tech can interrupt and ask questions anytime
  • Only speaks when confident. Says "shut up" and it backs off

A senior tech looking over your shoulder on every single job.

Nothing else on the market does this. ChatGPT can't watch a camera feed. Google can't speak up when it sees a problem. This is a new category.
