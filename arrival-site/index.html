Getting Started

Creating your account

Download Arrival from the App Store or Google Play and sign up with your email. Start your 7-day free trial — no credit card required. During signup, select your primary trade (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, or general construction) so Arrival can tailor responses to your field.

Your first question

Tap the microphone button and ask a question like you'd ask a colleague: "What's the refrigerant charge for a Carrier 25HNH5?" or "What wire gauge do I need for a 40-amp circuit at 150 feet?" Arrival will respond with a sourced answer you can listen to hands-free or read on screen.

Using the camera

Point your phone's camera at any piece of equipment, wiring, or component and tap the camera button. Arrival will identify what it sees and let you ask follow-up questions about it. Works great for reading model numbers, identifying parts, and diagnosing visible issues.

Conversation history

Every conversation is saved automatically. Swipe right or tap the menu icon to see past conversations. Pro and Business users can search their full history, bookmark important answers, and share conversations with teammates.

Customising your experience

Head to Settings to adjust voice speed, toggle between listen and read modes, set your preferred units (metric or imperial), and configure notification preferences. You can also add your company's specific equipment inventory for more targeted answers.

Voice & Camera

Push-to-talk voice

Press and hold the mic button to talk, release when done. Arrival is built for noisy job sites — our voice engine filters out background noise from compressors, power tools, and HVAC systems. Speak naturally, like you're talking to a colleague. You don't need to use specific keywords or commands.

Tips for better voice results

Be specific: "What's the torque spec for the compressor bolts on a Trane XR15?" will get a better answer than "what's the torque spec?". Include model numbers, brand names, and context about what you're working on. If an answer isn't quite right, ask a follow-up to narrow it down.

Camera identification

Tap the camera icon to snap a photo. Arrival can identify equipment makes and models, read data plates and labels, recognise wiring configurations, identify pipe types and fittings, and read diagnostic codes from control boards. For best results, make sure the subject is well-lit and in focus.

Combining voice and camera

The most powerful way to use Arrival is together: snap a photo of the equipment, then ask a question about it. "What's wrong with this?" while pointing at a circuit board gives Arrival visual context plus your question, resulting in much more accurate diagnostics.

Audio responses

Arrival reads answers aloud through your phone speaker or connected Bluetooth earpiece. Adjust voice speed in Settings. Perfect for when your hands are occupied with tools or equipment and you need the answer read back to you.

Proactive Mode

What is Proactive Mode?

Proactive Mode is a Business plan exclusive feature. Instead of waiting for you to ask a question, Arrival watches what you're working on and speaks up with relevant information automatically. It's like having a master technician looking over your shoulder, catching things before they become problems.

How it works

When Proactive Mode is enabled, Arrival monitors the context of your current job — the equipment you're working on, the questions you've asked, and the photos you've taken. Based on this context, it proactively surfaces safety warnings, common mistakes, relevant code requirements, and suggested next steps without you having to ask.

What Proactive Mode can do

Alert you to common mistakes for the specific equipment you're servicing. Surface relevant safety warnings and code requirements automatically. Suggest next steps based on your current diagnostic path. Flag when a job matches a known recall or service bulletin. Learn from your team's most common errors and help prevent them.

Enabling Proactive Mode

Business account administrators can enable Proactive Mode for their team from the Admin Dashboard under Settings. Individual technicians can toggle Proactive Mode on or off from their app settings. When enabled, you'll see a small indicator at the top of the screen showing that Proactive Mode is active.

Privacy and control

Proactive Mode only uses the context of your current session — it doesn't monitor you outside of active jobs. You can dismiss any proactive suggestion with a swipe, and you can turn it off at any time. Administrators can see aggregate data on how often proactive suggestions are triggered and accepted, but cannot see individual technician sessions.

Troubleshooting

Voice not being recognised

Make sure Arrival has microphone permissions enabled in your phone settings. If you're on a noisy site, hold the phone closer to your mouth or use a Bluetooth earpiece. Try restarting the app if voice stops working mid-session. Check that you're pressing and holding the mic button (not just tapping).

Camera not identifying equipment

Ensure the camera has good lighting — dark mechanical rooms can make identification difficult. Try to capture the data plate or model label clearly. Clean your camera lens (trade sites get dusty). If identification fails, you can always type or speak the model number instead.

Slow or no responses

Check your internet connection — Arrival needs a stable connection to work. If you're in an area with poor signal, try moving to a spot with better reception. Slow responses can also occur during peak usage times. Business users get priority routing which significantly reduces wait times.

Inaccurate answers

While Arrival is highly accurate, always verify critical information with official manufacturer documentation and local codes. If you receive an answer that seems wrong, tap the flag icon to report it. Provide context about what was incorrect and we'll review and improve the model. Remember, Arrival is an assistive tool and doesn't replace professional judgement.

App crashing or freezing

Force-close the app and reopen it. Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store or Google Play. If crashes persist, go to Settings, then Advanced, then Clear Cache. If the problem continues, contact support@arrival.ai with your device model and OS version so we can investigate.
