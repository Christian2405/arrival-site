Getting Started

Creating your account

Download Arrival from the App Store or Google Play. Sign up with your email and select your trade. The free plan gives you 10 questions a day with voice and camera — no credit card required.

Your first question

Hold the mic button and ask something. "What refrigerant does this unit take?" or "What wire gauge for a 40-amp circuit at 150 feet?" Arrival answers out loud so you can keep working.

Using the camera

Tap the camera icon. Point it at a unit, label, or wiring and ask a follow-up question about what you see. Good for model numbers, identifying parts, and diagnosing visible issues.

Conversation history

Every conversation is saved. Swipe right or tap the menu icon to see past conversations.

Settings

Adjust voice speed, toggle between listen and read modes, and set your preferred units in Settings.

Voice & Camera

Push-to-talk voice

Hold the mic button, talk, release. Arrival filters background noise from compressors, tools, and HVAC systems. Speak naturally — no keywords or commands needed.

Tips for better results

Be specific. "What's the torque spec on the compressor bolts for a Trane XR15?" beats "what's the torque spec?" Include model numbers and context. If an answer isn't right, ask a follow-up.

Camera

Tap the camera icon to snap a photo. Arrival can read data plates, identify wiring, recognize pipe types, and read diagnostic codes. Make sure the subject is well-lit and in focus.

Voice + camera together

Snap a photo, then ask about it. "What's wrong with this?" while pointing at a circuit board gives Arrival visual context plus your question.

Audio responses

Arrival reads answers out loud. Works through your phone speaker or Bluetooth earpiece. Adjust speed in Settings.

Job Mode

What is Job Mode?

Job Mode is a Business-only feature. Instead of waiting for you to ask, Arrival watches what you're working on through the camera and speaks up when it sees something worth flagging.

How it works

Enable Job Mode and point the camera at your work. Arrival monitors the feed and surfaces safety warnings, common mistakes, and code requirements automatically — without you having to ask.

What it can do

Flag common mistakes for the equipment you're servicing. Surface safety warnings and code requirements. Suggest next steps based on what it sees. Alert you to known recalls or service bulletins.

Enabling Job Mode

Admins can enable Job Mode for their team from the dashboard. Individual techs can toggle it on or off in the app. A small indicator at the top of the screen shows when Job Mode is active.

Privacy

Job Mode only uses the current session context. It doesn't monitor you outside of active jobs. You can dismiss any alert with a swipe and turn it off at any time.

Troubleshooting

Voice not recognized

Check that Arrival has microphone permissions in your phone settings. On noisy sites, hold the phone closer or use a Bluetooth earpiece. Make sure you're pressing and holding the mic button, not just tapping.

Camera not identifying equipment

Make sure there's good lighting. Try to capture the data plate or label clearly. Clean your camera lens. If identification fails, type or speak the model number instead.

Slow or no responses

Arrival needs a stable internet connection. If you're in a weak signal area, move to a better spot. Business users get priority routing.

Inaccurate answers

Always verify critical information with official sources and local codes. If an answer seems wrong, tap the flag icon to report it. Arrival is an assistive tool — it doesn't replace professional judgment.

App crashing

Force-close and reopen. Make sure you're on the latest version. If it persists, go to Settings > Advanced > Clear Cache. Still broken? Email support@arrivalcompany.com with your device model and OS version.

Arrival — Account Deletion

How to delete your Arrival account

You can delete your account in two ways:

Option 1: From the app

Open Arrival → Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2: By email

Send an email to privacy@arrivalcompany.com from the email address associated with your account. Include "Delete my account" in the subject line. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, trade, experience level)
  • Your query history and saved answers
  • Your uploaded documents and manuals
  • Your video clips and spatial intelligence data (if recording was enabled)
  • Your subscription and billing relationship (active subscriptions are cancelled)
  • Your team membership (if applicable)

What is retained

Anonymized, aggregated usage data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for service improvement purposes. This data contains no personal information.

Retention period

All identifiable data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. Backups are purged within 90 days.
