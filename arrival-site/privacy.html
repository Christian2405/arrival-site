Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Arrival AI ("Arrival," "we," "our," or "us") operates the Arrival AI mobile application and the website at arrivalcompany.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials (managed securely through Supabase).

Usage Data: We collect questions you ask, responses provided, feedback ratings, and general usage patterns to improve the service.

Camera and Microphone Data: When you use voice or camera features, audio and images are processed in real-time to generate responses. We do not permanently store raw audio recordings. Camera frames are temporarily held in memory during active sessions and discarded when the session ends.

Spatial Intelligence Data: When you opt in to spatial data collection, camera frames captured during Job Mode sessions may be stored to build spatial awareness of your work environment. This data includes camera frames, timestamps, and associated AI context. Spatial data is stored securely and is only used to improve the guidance you receive during active job sessions. You can enable or disable spatial data collection at any time in your account settings. Spatial data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

Uploaded Documents: Documents you upload (service manuals, spec sheets, building plans) are processed, indexed, and stored to power your personalized knowledge base.

Device Information: We collect basic device information (operating system, app version) for crash reporting and performance monitoring through Sentry.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Arrival AI service
  • To generate accurate, trade-specific responses to your questions
  • To process voice commands and camera-based diagnostics
  • To index and search your uploaded documents
  • To monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve response quality

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers, solely to operate the service:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Your questions and images are sent to generate AI responses
  • Deepgram: Audio is sent for speech-to-text transcription
  • ElevenLabs: Text is sent for text-to-speech conversion
  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • Pinecone: Vector storage for document search
  • Sentry: Crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • LiveKit: Real-time voice communication infrastructure

Each provider processes data according to their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

4. Team Features

If you join a team, documents uploaded to the team knowledge base are accessible to all team members. Your individual questions and responses are not shared with your team.

5. Data Retention

Your account data and uploaded documents are retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual documents or your entire account at any time. Query logs are retained for service improvement and may be anonymized after 90 days.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), secure authentication tokens, and access controls. All API communications are encrypted in transit.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Delete individual uploaded documents
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

8. Children's Privacy

Arrival is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
